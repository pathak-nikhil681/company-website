Frequently Asked Questions

01 What is the purpose of application maintenance in the realm of software development?

+

Application maintenance in software development is akin to system upkeep. It involves ongoing monitoring, debugging, updating, and enhancing software applications to ensure their continuous functionality, security, and relevance. Just as machinery requires regular maintenance for optimal performance, software applications demand continuous attention to adapt to changing requirements, fix bugs, and incorporate technological advancements. At Hashtechy, we prioritise these technical aspects, ensuring your applications remain robust, secure, and aligned with evolving business needs.

02 What does the term 'Website Maintenance' encompass in the context of online platforms?

+

Website maintenance involves systematically monitoring, updating, and managing a website to uphold its peak performance, security, and relevance. Analogous to the care given to any valuable asset, continuous attention is essential for a website to operate efficiently. This includes content updates, bug fixes, implementing security patches, optimising performance, and integrating new features as necessary.

At Hashtechy, our specialised website maintenance services cater to these technical requirements, ensuring your website remains current, secure, and aligned with your business objectives. Our expert team strives to provide users with a seamless and engaging online experience by keeping your website up-to-date and fully functional.

03 What is 'application maintenance and support' involved in software development?

+

In software development, 'application maintenance and support' encompasses the ongoing processes conducted after deploying a software application. It includes tasks like regular updates, bug fixes, feature enhancements, and providing technical assistance to ensure the continued smooth operation and alignment with changing user needs. Maintenance addresses routine activities such as bug fixes and performance optimisation. At the same time, support involves customer assistance, troubleshooting, and issue resolution, ensuring the application's reliability, efficiency, and user satisfaction throughout its lifecycle.
