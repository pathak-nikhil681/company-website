Frequently Asked Questions

01 How can I find the right custom mobile app development company?

+

When you're picking a mobile app development company from a bunch of options, it helps to think about a few things:

  • Experience: Check if the company has done similar projects before and has a good history.
  • Expertise: Make sure they know what they're doing in mobile app development.
  • Portfolio: Look at what they've worked on before to see if it fits what you want.
  • Quality: A good company cares about making apps without any bugs. Check if they have a focus on quality.
  • Support: Find out if they help you even after launching the app.
  • Cost: See if their prices match your budget.
  • Reputation: Do research see what others say about them, read reviews, and ask for references.
  • Project Management: Look for a company that manages projects well, so things stay organised.
  • Trust: Go for an open company that works with you and gets what you want.

    • By thinking about these things, you can find a great mobile app development company like Hashtechy that fits your needs and makes working together successful.

02 How much time and money do I need to create a Mobile App?

+

Creating a mobile app takes different amounts of time and money based on a few things.

  • Time: Simple apps with basic stuff might take 2-3 months. If your app is more complicated with fancy features, it could take 4-6 months or even longer.
  • Cost: The money depends on how much the development team charges per hour, how big your project is, and if you need any extra services. For a basic app, you might spend a few thousand dollars. But if your app is complicated, it could cost several hundred thousand dollars.

It's smart to connect with a good mobile app development company to determine exactly how much time and money your app needs. At Hashtechy, we understand your needs and give you a clear plan.
